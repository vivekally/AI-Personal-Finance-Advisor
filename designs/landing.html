Tax Advantage

Every dollar in the right account
saves you 20–40% in tax.

RRSP, TFSA, and FHSA are Canada's three superpower accounts. Most newcomers don't know how to compare them — or that the FHSA gives you the deduction and the tax-free withdrawal.

TFSA — Tax-Free Savings
Growth is 100% tax-free
Contribute after-tax dollars. Everything that grows and everything you withdraw — all tax-free, forever.
$7,000/year × 25 years at 7%
≈ $443,000 tax-free
FHSA — First Home
Best of both worlds
Deductible going in (like RRSP), tax-free coming out (like TFSA). The most powerful account Canada offers — but you must open it by Dec 31.
$8,000 deducted at 30% marginal rate
$2,400 tax refund — year 1
RRSP — Retirement
Tax deduction now
Reduce your taxable income today. Pay tax later when you withdraw — typically at a lower retirement-age rate. Year 1 = $0 for newcomers; unlocks after first tax return.
$10,000 contributed at 30% rate
$3,000 tax refund
