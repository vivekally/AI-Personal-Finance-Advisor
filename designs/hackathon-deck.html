Slide 6 · Responsible AI & Trust by Design · 15%
Built Responsibly
Trust isn't a feature. It's the architecture.
An AI that holds opinions about $100,000 of someone's life savings must be auditable, accountable, and humble about what it doesn't know.
📚
"Education, not advice"
Every wizard output is labeled as personalized education. Avoids triggering OSC/provincial Securities Act requirements for registered investment advice. Fintech-lawyer scoping call booked before Stripe launch.
👤
Human-in-the-loop escalation
AI generates a brief → licensed CFP reviews and annotates → user executes. No autonomous trades. Every recommendation has a human signature before any money moves.
🇨🇦
PIPEDA + data residency
Turso SQLite in ca-central-1 (Montreal). Flinks & SnapTrade tokens AES-256 encrypted at the application layer. Zero raw account data in plaintext logs — verified by audit trail.
📩
CASL-compliant WhatsApp
Double opt-in. Timestamp + IP stored. STOP keyword unsubscribes immediately and purges from the Dec 14 reminder queue. Non-compliance penalty: up to $10M — we won't risk it.
📊
Every claim has a source
13 primary sources cited throughout: IRCC, FCAC, Statista, DLA Piper, Flinks, FP Canada. Estimates are labeled as estimates. Banks' #1 new-customer segment? Globe and Mail, on the record.
🚫
Brokerage execution = pending
SnapTrade order execution is deferred to Year 2 pending IIROC/CSA review. Until cleared, we surface "guided execution" — user reviews and confirms in their own broker. We don't pretend to be what we're not.

🎯 What nobody else is shipping
Newcomer-first edge-case logic
RRSP year-1 = $0. TFSA from tax residency. FHSA Dec 31 deadline as a permanent loss. Student tax-residency eligibility. Impossible to bolt onto a generic finance app — requires ground-up architecture.
10-language plan, multi-script done right
Most products: English only, or English + French. We commit to the actual newcomer language mix — and ship multi-script typography that respects Devanagari, Gurmukhi, Arabic-script (RTL), Mandarin.
🔥 The bank acquisition channel
Banks already pay $200–550 CAC per newcomer (RBC's $400 cash + Apple Watch bundle is documented). Arrive Finance ships them already-educated, ready-to-open customers. $50–150 referral fee = 70–80% bank CAC discount + a B2B revenue stream on top of consumer SaaS.
Human-in-the-loop AI for regulated advice
Robo-advisors execute autonomously. Self-directed brokerages give no guidance. We sit in the middle: AI generates the brief, licensed CFP reviews, user executes. The safety net no competitor provides.
